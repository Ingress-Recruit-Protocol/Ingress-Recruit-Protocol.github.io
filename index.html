Throughout human history, portals for Exotic Matter have been the main source of social, cultural and scientific progress. The Enlightened view XM as a gift. Destiny. A way for Humanity to reach a new state of being through its mind-enhancing properties. The Enlightened fight the Resistance to free XM and welcome the Shaper influence as a boon to EVOLVE humanity.

Earth has been invaded and influneced by Exotic Matter portals for too long. Humanity is being Shaped towards unknown ends by extra-dimensional beings. The Resistance view XM with caution. A potential threat of unknown origin. Something of mysterious intent that must be controlled and studied. The Resistance fight the Enlightened and use Exotic Matter against the Shaper influence in order to PROTECT humanity.
